About Me

Hello! My name is Keno Osima, and I am a data analyst dedicated to transforming raw data into valuable insights that drive informed decision-making. With a strong foundation in administrative support and a detail-oriented mindset, I've honed the ability to organize, analyze, and communicate data effectively. My career journey began in administrative and operations roles where I developed a deep appreciation for process optimization and the power of data in improving workflows and outcomes.

Over time, I transitioned into data analytics, where I now focus on leveraging tools like Excel, SQL, and Power BI to uncover patterns, create compelling visualizations, and provide actionable recommendations. I thrive on the challenge of solving complex problems and transforming abstract numbers into stories that resonate with diverse audiences, from executives to operational teams.

Based in Calgary, Alberta, I bring a unique blend of analytical expertise and operational insight to my work, enabling me to bridge the gap between technical data solutions and real-world applications. Whether I'm cleaning and standardizing datasets, building dynamic dashboards, or collaborating with stakeholders to identify key business opportunities, I am committed to delivering impactful results.

Beyond the numbers, I'm passionate about continuous learning and growth, staying up-to-date with emerging trends and technologies in data analytics. My goal is to help organizations make sense of their data and use it as a strategic asset for success.
